Here are some of my favourites!

TV SHOWS AND MOVIES

Avatar the Last Airbender

If you haven't watched this you are missing out big time. This is my favourite TV show, and I rewatch it once every six months. The storyline is captivating and the characters are amazing. A fantasic series that has some very important themes, strong female characters and beautiful friendships. WATCH IT!

The Dragon Prince

When I started watching this show, the first thing that caught my eye was the animation. It is stunning. I loved the story and adventure in this series. Definitely a great watch!

Taare Zameen Par

(Hindi)

A heartwrenching bollywood film starring Aamir Khan and Amole Gupte. It follows Ishaan who is sent away to boarding school where he meets Ram, an art teacher.

Howl's Moving Castle

A Studio Ghibli masterpiece. That is all. Watch it.

The Legend of Korra

Set 70 years after Avatar the Last Airbender following the next avatar, Korra, who is a waterbender. Like Avatar it's a wonderful series focusing controversial themes as well.

BOOKS

Atomic Habits by James Clear

This book is about creating good habits and breaking the bad ones. The writing is very easy to understand and follow. Great read if you're looking to become more productive.

The Kite Runner by Khaled Hosseini

I read this for the first time when I was in high school. This is a must read! Khaled Hosseini is an amazing storyteller. The story follows the friendship of two boys from two different financial backgrounds in Afghanistan. It leaves you awestruck when you finish reading it.

A Conjuring of Light by V.E. Schwab

This is the third book in the Shades of Magic series by V.E. Schwab. The last book is by far my favourite. It is beautifully written and the storyline is magical.

The Infernal Devices series by Cassandra Clare

This series is undoubtly my favourite book series of all time. I was not the biggest fan of The Mortal Instruments series by Cassandra Clare, however this one was wonderful. You are bound to fall in love with the characters and the story.

Lovely War by Julie Berry

A story set in WWI, told by Greek gods but mainly the goddess Aphrodite. This one is probably a book romance lovers will like!
